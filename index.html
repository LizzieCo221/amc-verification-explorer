Data sources and methodology
  • AMC Arms Control Agreement Database V2 — Alva Myrdal Centre, Uppsala University
  • Risk classification derived from the item column (summary_category = 0) in amcdata_weapons_facilities_V2.csv using keyword matching on nuclear, chemical, biological, ICBM, SLBM, ballistic missile, and related terms
  • All six variables normalized to 0–1 before weighting, then rescaled to 0–100. Each slider multiplies the normalized variable by a weight of 0–5
  • † Manually coded (no real item rows in dataset): JCPOA, Quadripartite Agreement, Brazil-Argentina Coop → high risk; Open Skies, CFE Adaptation, BDA Agreement, Sub-Regional Arms Control → low risk
  • 97 of 128 agreements have zero verified compliance mechanisms and are excluded from this visualization
